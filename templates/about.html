Our Mission

Our mission is to create a platform where people who are diagnosed with
mental health illness or have drug addiction can meet each other to make each individual feel
less alone about their situation. Our project aims to achieve our goal with our sorting
mechanism that we call "filters" that filter common activities such as hobbies, academics to
make the experience less awkward. After people have been grouped with a group generator,
online activities and "lessons" and experts on the topic of drugs and addiction can help educate
these people to help cope with their situation.
